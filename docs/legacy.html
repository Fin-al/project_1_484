From Consumer to Creator

Before I saw the Grid, technology felt like a closed box. The film’s vision of a Digital Frontier changed that. It didn't just show me a movie; it showed me a world where I could be the architect.

I used to look at a screen and see a static interface. Now, I see a limitless landscape of clusters and freeways. That opening monologue—the part about dreaming of a world you'd never see—hit me personally because it described exactly how I felt about the future of the internet.

The Art of the Code

The 3D high-tech adventure of *Tron* proved that math and logic don't have to be cold. They can be beautiful. By picturing information as physical light and motion, the movie made me realize that coding is a form of digital art.
